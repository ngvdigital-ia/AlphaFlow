How It Works in Practice

The student learns how to understand a strategy in which, after selecting stocks from companies considered more solid, those positions may be used in operations intended to generate premiums/income from those assets.

The purpose is not simply to teach someone to buy stocks and wait for them to go up. The purpose is to show how certain stocks can be used within an income-focused strategy, with a clearer logic and a more defined process.

To make it simple: if a course teaches how to invest in fixed-income products such as CDs or bonds, it is a fixed-income investing course. Here, the idea is the same — this is a stock investing course focused on generating income through a specific strategy applied to those assets.
